About Me

Paige Lieske is a 22-year veteran of the "office world". Her considerable background in office management, purchasing, inventory management, database and peripheral management, and customer service, has allowed her the unique perspective of living every part of the process. It has made her a valuable asset to her current employer but alas, it is time to move on.

Having managed 4 database conversions and numerous acquisitions in the past 10 years, Paige has always loved the process of writing and evaluating the necessary code to make the database run properly. Whether it be strictly out of the box, or company specific modifications, she has taken every challenge and created a robust and functional environment for her company to flourish. This desire to create and learn has led her to seek out web development and coding as the next step in her career.

For examples of Paige's work, or to get more information, please visit her GitHub at https://github.com/paigelieske, or email paigeproctor@comcast.net. Paige currently resides in Peachtree Corners, Georgia, with her husband, son, and cat.
